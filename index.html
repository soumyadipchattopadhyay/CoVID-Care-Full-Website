What is CoVID Care and What Facilities Does It Provide?

CoVID Care is an android application and an website developed for the citizen of India to provide them many urgent and essential services during the lockdown and pandemic situation in India caused by n-CoV.The services have been grouped into five main segments or modules which are as follows :

  • Home Screen
  • Government Services
  • Medical Services
  • Regional Services
  • Other Facilities
There are many more facilities present in the mentioned segments which provides the user more details and information related to the situations prevailing in the surround

Live Data



Using this website and application, one can get 24x7 updated latest data of CoVID-19 Affected people in their state and in the whole country. Several trustworthy resources provided by Government of India have been used to keep the transparency of the data.

24x7 Helpline and WhatsApp BOT

We have provided 24x7 Government Helpline Numbers and First Responders Numbers in the apps and websites. So that in case of any emergency, you do not need to wander and search for emergency numbers. Also we have added MyGOV and State-wise Customised WhatsApp Helplines to get virtual help

Health Monitoring and Medical Facilities

Keepin the status of health of the user in mind, we have developed and added numerous health related services like BMI and BMR Calculation, Heart Rate Measurement, Nearby Testing Labs, Blood Bank Contacts and many more. These modules will help the user to monitor their health by staying at home

And Many More Services and Facilities

In the websites and applications we have provided many more services relevant to CoVID situation like :

  • CoVID-19 FAQs
  • About CoVID-19
  • Fitness Videos
  • News Authenticity
